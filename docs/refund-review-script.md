# EuroCoin Refund Service Overview

## Executive Summary

- **Product focus:** EuroCoin Refunds delivers a compliance-ready refund management experience blending on-chain transparency with enterprise workflows.
- **Customer promise:** Faster refund approvals, auditable records, real-time client comms, and a single pane of glass for finance, legal, and support teams.
- **Differentiators:** Wallet-native onboarding, AML-aware automation, dual-channel notifications (email + Telegram), and multilingual UX.

## Platform Highlights

- **Wallet-first onboarding:** Customers authenticate with their existing wallet; balances, refund eligibility, and token tax logic load instantly.
- **Guided refund requests:** A localized form captures refund intent, supporting documents, and routes the ticket to the correct department (Finance, Legal, Support).
- **Compliance-ready workflows:** Every refund is tracked with status history, audit logs, and automatic alerts for legal review or AML flags.
- **Operations cockpit:** Admins receive Telegram notifications, gain a unified dashboard with KYC insights, and can drill into delay timers, smart receipts, and transaction proofs.
- **Customer communications:** Automated emails (Resend), verified newsletter flows, and AI-assisted chat keep clients informed and reduce support load.

## Target Audience

- Fintechs managing tokenized refunds or cash-back programs.
- Internal treasury or operations teams that require provable audit trails.
- Legal/compliance teams needing real-time visibility into refund cases.
